Ethical Use

This extension is intended for privacy-preserving noise, QA/staging validation, and educational research. Do not use it for ad fraud, engagement inflation, revenue manipulation, or bypassing consent barriers.
  • Operate only on domains you own, have permission to test, or explicitly allow.
  • Avoid authenticated flows unless you opt in and understand the risk.
  • Keep interaction rates humane to avoid generating harmful load.
